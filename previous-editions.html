The International Conference on Synthesis, Modeling, Analysis and Simulation Methods and Applications to Circuit Design Conference has its origins in the “International Workshop on Symbolic Methods and Applications to Circuit Design”:

  • 1991: Bagneux, France.
  • 1992: Firenze, Italy.
  • 1994: Seville, Spain.
  • 1996: Leuven, Belgium.
  • 1998: Kaiserslautern, Germany.
  • 2000: Lisboa, Portugal.
  • 2002: Sinaia, Romania.
  • 2004: Wroclaw, Poland.
  • 2006: Firenze, Italy.

With the updated scope and name, four editions took place in:

  • 2008: Erfurt, Germany.
  • 2010: Gammarth, Tunisia.
  • 2012: Seville, Spain.
  • 2015: Istanbul, Turkey.

Since 20016, SMACD has been colocated with PRIME bringing Circuit designers d EDA developers together.

  • 2016: Lisbon, Portugal.
  • 2017: Giardini Naxos - Taormina, Italy.
  • 2018: Prague, Czech Republic.
  • 2019: Lausanne, Switzerland.
  • 2021: Erfurt, Germany (Online Event).
