SMACD 2023 SPONSOR PACKAGES

The SMACD 2023 organization makes available 4 sponsor packages, respectively, Diamond (exclusive to 3 sponsors), Platinum, Gold and Silver. The 4 sponsor packages include different company involvement on the conference program, conference committees, conference awards, conference social events and exhibition space, please check the table below.

Conference Program:

  • Propose a Keynote Lecture (60')
  • Propose a Tutorial (90' )
  • Pitch on the Industrial Session (10')

Conference Awards:

  • EDA Award
  • IC Design Award
  • Best Paper Award

Conference Committee:

  • Propose 1 to 2 TPC Members

Exhibition Space:

  • Table & Chairs, Basic Electricity, Wi-Fi
  • 2 to 4 Full Registrations (FR)
  • 2 to 4 3-day Accommodation (AC) at the 5* VidaMar Resort Hotel and Congress Centre

Company Logo on:

  • USB for the Proceedings (provided by the company), Signs at the Gala Dinner, Acknowledgments by Conference Chair (ULTRA)
  • Signs at Welcome Cocktail + Acknowledgments by Conference Chair (MAX)
  • Banner at Conference Venue (PRO)
  • Website, Final Program, Proceedings, Ack. slide during Session Breaks (BASIC)
