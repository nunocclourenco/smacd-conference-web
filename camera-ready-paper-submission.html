CAMERA-READY PAPER AND COPYRIGHT FORM SUBMISSION

The steps required to finalize the upload process of papers are described in the following paragraphs:

Proceed with registration

As a first step, you must register for the conference.

DO NOT FORGET to finalize your registration: mandatory camera-ready paper & copyright form submission are expected!

PLEASE NOTE that any accepted paper included in the final program is expected to have at least one author attending and presenting the paper at the conference.

Authors of the accepted papers included in the final program who do not attend the Conference will be subscribed to a “No Show List”. The “No Show List” will also be made available to the following SMACD conference organizers, who can reject submissions from these authors in the following years, based on their past negative impact on the conference.

Camera-ready paper submission

While preparing the final version of your paper, please, make sure to read the reviews of your paper and take into account the reviewers' recommendations for revising it.

Each accepted manuscript should not exceed four (4) IEEE style pages.

Copyright Clearance Code Notice

The appropriate copyright clearance code notice is to appear on the bottom of the first page of each paper according to the guidelines set forth in the Cataloging/Copyright Instructions for an IEEE Conference Proceeding.

  • For papers in which all authors are employed by the US government, the copyright notice is:
         U.S. Government work not protected by U.S. copyright
  • For papers in which all authors are employed by a Crown government (UK, Canada, and Australia), the copyright notice is:
        979-8-3503-3265-0/23/$31.00 ©2023 Crown
  • For papers in which all authors are employed by the European Union, the copyright notice is:
        979-8-3503-3265-0/23/$31.00 ©2023 European Union
  • For all other papers the copyright notice is:
        979-8-3503-3265-0/23/$31.00 ©2023 IEEE

Instructions to Use PDF eXpress:

  1. Go to the IEEE PDF eXpress login page and register your paper. Conference ID: 58065X.
  2. Full conference name: 2023 19th International Conference on Synthesis, Modeling, Analysis and Simulation Methods and Applications to Circuit Design (SMACD)
  3. If you are a new user click on “New Users – Click here” and fill in your information. You’ll need the conference ID 58065X to apply for the account.
  4. Follow on-site instruction for using IEEE PDF eXpress and make sure your PDF has been approved with clean compliance report.

Upload the paper's proceedings version

  1. After using the IEEE PDF eXpress system for file compliance, return to the SMACD's final submission web site (EasyChair)
  2. Click on the “access Final Version” button of your submission, or optionally, on the top right button “Proceedings version” if already inside the submission.
  3. Use the “Upload new version” button to upload the PDF generated by the IEEE PDF eXpress system.
  4. Authors are required to consent and transfer the manuscript's copyright according to IEEE standard norms.
  5. Complete the “Sign Copyright Agreement” steps. When proceeding, kindly save the PDF of the Copyright Agreement.

Measures against plagiarism

A paper detected with plagiarism will be excluded from the conference proceedings and IEEE Xplore.
In addition, the authors' names could go to the black list of IEEE for detected papers with plagiarism.

It is the responsibility of the author(s) to ensure that all conditions for paper publishing are met.
