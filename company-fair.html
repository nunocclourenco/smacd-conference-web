Company Fair

The SMACD 2022 and PRIME 2022 Company Fair will be held from the 12th to the 15th of June 2022 in Villasimius, Sardinia, Italy, at the Tanka Village Resort.

The company fair is organized to allow academia researchers and Ph.D. students, attending to both conferences (SMACD 2019 and PRIME 2019) in the areas of IC Design and Electronic Design Automation, to get in contact with industries and other research institutions (and vice-versa) to promote networking and identify future opportunities and collaborations.

For more information contact us at info@smacd-conference.org.

Sponsorship

  • 2000 EUR CONFERENCE BRONZE SPONSORSHIP
    This Patronage level includes: a full registration for 1 person; the facilities for a company both in the Company Fair (table, chairs and space to place marketing materials); the inclusion of the company logo on the conference website and conference materials; the addition of a paragraph about the company on the website of the conferences and final program; the acknowledgment by the General Chairs during the Opening, Closing and Awards Ceremonies.
  • 2500 EUR CONFERENCE SILVER SPONSORSHIP
    In addition to the benefits of the Bronze Sponsorship, this Patronage Level includes the full registration for a second person.
  • 3000 EUR CONFERENCE GOLD SPONSORSHIP
    In addition to the benefits of a Silver Sponsorship, a third full registration is granted.
