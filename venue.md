## Conference Venue
# Tanka Village
The conference venue is the Tanka Village, a marvelous resort in Villasimius, on the coast of south Sardinia. Tanka Village features a variety of sports facilities, including five tennis courts, football pitches, bowling greens, volleyball courts and a gym. Furthermore it features a private beach, five restaurants (including one poolside) and six bars.

With different locations around the resort, accommodation ranges from rooms to suites to bungalows. All air-conditioned, they also feature a fridge, satellite TV and a balcony or patio. Moreover, Tanka Village features a large olympic-size swimming pool, as well as a shallow water area including a waterfall. The resort overlooks the stretch of coast from Simius beach to Porto Giunco, connecting two of the most beautiful beaches in southern Sardinia. The centre of Villasimius is 1 km from Tanka Village, while Cagliari Elmas Airport can be reached in approximately 1-hour drive.
