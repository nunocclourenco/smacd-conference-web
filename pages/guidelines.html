Oral Presentations

  • The duration of oral presentations will be 15 minutes (20 minutes for the EDA Competition presentations).
  • A PC will be available in each room of the conference venue for the presentations of each session. Presenters are requested to upload their presentations to the room PC at least 10 minutes before the start of their session.
  • Supported presentation files are .ppt and .pdf. You can find a logo of SMACD 2025 for your presentation here.
  • Presenters are requested to familiarize themselves with the podium and the room equipment in advance of their presentation. The members of the Conference Organization will be available to help throughout the conference.
  • Presenters are kindly asked to fill in their information in the following form, so that they are properly introduced by the Chair of their session.
