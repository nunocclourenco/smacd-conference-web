Turkish Cuisine:

Turkey's culinary scene is celebrated worldwide for its refinement and diversity, highlighting fresh, seasonal ingredients and regional specialties. In addition to famous grilled meat dishes (kebap, döner, etc.), be sure to sample the fresh fish from the Bosphorus and the iconic sweets like Baklava and various milk-based desserts.

As part of the authentic Turkish dining experience, every meal is ideally concluded with a cup of freshly brewed Turkish tea or aromatic Turkish coffee. Being a cosmopolitan city, Istanbul also offers a myriad of restaurants featuring international and fusion cuisines that are worth exploring. For more in-depth information on Turkish cuisine, visit the relevant Wikipedia entry.
