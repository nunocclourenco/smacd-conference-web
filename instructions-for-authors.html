the SMACD and PRIME conferences are approaching.
We therefore would like to draw your attention to the following information:

1. Schedule

Please, verify in the program the time slot allocated to your presentation.

2. Record your video presentation

Some important instructions about how to record your video presentation. Please follow them carefully.

  • Your video presentation should be max. 15 min long.
  • Please provide your recording in the format 16:9 landscape.
  • Videos should always be submitted in MP4 format with a resolution of 720p (1280 x 720 pixel) to 1080p (1920 x 1080 pixel). For best results, we recommend a constant bit rate of 60 fps (frames per second.
  • Name your file as Session ID-Author Name e.g., SMACD-A4.1_Georg or PRIME-A.1.2_Georg (check in the program).

There are numerous software solutions that help you create a professional-looking screen recording. We can recommend two tools that most speakers will likely have installed on their PC or Mac.

  • If you have created your presentation using PowerPoint, you can also use the built-in PowerPoint feature that lets you record an on-screen presentation with voiceover narration and camera input. The video narration feature, however, only works when you are using a Windows OS. :-(Users of other OS may have to use additional tools that allow them to add their camera feed to the recording. You can also follow instructions at: https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/office/turn-your-presentation-into-a-video-c140551f-cb37-4818-b5d4-3e30815c3e83 )
  • Another useful tool worth consideration the video conferencing platform Zoom. Its free version offers extensive options to record one’s own screen with a separate window showing the webcam view. You can find more information on Zoom’s local recording feature here: https://support.zoom.us/hc/en-us/articles/201362473-Local-recording
  • Another nice software tool to record your presentation you can find here: https://obsproject.com

TECHNICAL ADVICE: CREATING YOUR ACOUNT – How to LOGIN in hopin

Please log in using the following link:

https://hopin.com/events/smacd-prime-2021-briefing

Select your category (Speaker/Session Chair) and confirm with the "Checkout" button.

  1. If you don’t have a hopin account yet, click on Sign-up in order to create an account by entering your first and last name, your email address and creating a password.
  2. Press “Sign up with e-mail” and enter the online tool. If you already have a hopin account, just click on “login”, enter your e-mail and password, and click on the button “login with e-mail”.
  3. After entering the online tool, click on your avatar in the upper right corner and then on "Edit profile".
