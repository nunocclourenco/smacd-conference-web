Important notice on author registration

  • Authors are required to register by April 3rd, 2023.
  • If no author is registered by April 3rd, 2023, the paper may be removed from the programme.
  • Author registration can only cover the presentation of max. 2 papers.
  • Please mention your paper ID when you register for the conference.

Payment of Conference Fee

Payment for registration must be made in Euro. The conference fee has to be fully paid in advance by credit card or bank transfer.

Cancellation

  • Before 17 April, 100% refund (an administration fee of EUR 150 is however due)
  • After 17 April, no refunds.
  • Substitutions are always accepted.
