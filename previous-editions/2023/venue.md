## Conference Venue
# Tanka Village
<br>
The conference venue is the Tanka Village, a marvelous resort in Villasimius, on the coast of south Sardinia. Tanka Village features a variety of sports facilities, including five tennis courts, football pitches, bowling greens, volleyball courts and a gym. Furthermore it features a private beach, five restaurants (including one poolside) and six bars.

<br>With different locations around the resort, accommodation ranges from rooms to suites to bungalows. All air-conditioned, they also feature a fridge, satellite TV and a balcony or patio. Moreover, Tanka Village features a large olympic-size swimming pool, as well as a shallow water area including a waterfall. The resort overlooks the stretch of coast from Simius beach to Porto Giunco, connecting two of the most beautiful beaches in southern Sardinia. The centre of Villasimius is 1 km from Tanka Village, while Cagliari Elmas Airport can be reached in approximately 1-hour drive.

For special offers for [**accommodation**](https://www.pragmacongressi.it/elenco-eventi/eventi-2022/12-15-june-primesmacd-2022/primesmacd-accommodation.html) for attendees, follow the [link](https://www.pragmacongressi.it/elenco-eventi/eventi-2022/12-15-june-primesmacd-2022/primesmacd-accommodation.html).

[**Transportation to TANKA VILLAGE**](https://www.pragmacongressi.it/primesmacd-shuttle-service.html): a shuttle service will be made available from/to Cagliari Airport at fixed times on arrival date (June 12th) and departure date (June 16th). Maximum estimated waiting time from your arrival in Cagliari Airpot to shuttle departure: 2 hrs.

If you wish to use this service, please fill in the [form](https://www.pragmacongressi.it/primesmacd-shuttle-service.html) with your your arrival/departure time and Tanka Resort reservation code.

The nearest airport to the conference venue is Cagliari (CAG), which is located at around 50 km from the Tanka Village (average travel time: 50 minutes). The cost of a private transportation (no group shuttle) is around 120&euro;

It is the responsibility of the attendee to arrange appropriate insurance cover in connection with their attendance at the conference.&nbsp;
